• Measure all-purpose flour and pour into large mixing bowl.
  • Slowly whisk in milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until the consistancy is smooth.
  • Lightly oil a griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  • Soak the bread slices into the milk mixture.
  • Cook the bread slices on the griddle or frying pan. Make sure each side of the bead is golden brown. Serve while still hot.
