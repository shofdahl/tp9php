• Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Butter 8" x 4" inch loaf pan.
  • Mash banannas with a fork in a large mixing bowl untill completely smooth.
  • Stir in melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  • Mix in baking soda and salt.
  • Stir in sugar, beatten egg, and vanilla extract until evenly distributed.
  • Mix in flour until combined.
  • Add and stir in chocolate chips, if desired.
  • Pour batter into loaf pan.
  • Bake for 55-65 minutes at 350 degrees F, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Remove from oven, and let cool for 5 minutes.
  • Cut, serve, and enjoy!
